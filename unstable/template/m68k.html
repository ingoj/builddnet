Other useful information:

  • http://wiki.debian.org/DebianM68kPorting - this page tracks current issues with the m68k port.
  • http://wiki.debian.org/DebianInstallerM68kTodo - this page tracks DebianInstaller development for the m68k port.
  • You can find some porters either on #debian-68k or on #debian-devel on irc.debian.org as well.

  • crest has the full range of chroots available to Debian Developers, kullervo only on request.
    The login message explains how to access them. Write the m68k-build list to have things installed/removed.

    • Note: it can take several minutes to connect with SSH2 to a 68060 equipped machine.
