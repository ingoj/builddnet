crest has the full range of chroots available to users. The login message explains how to access them. Write the m68k-build list or me to have things installed/removed.
Note: it can take several minutes to connect with SSH2 to a 68060 equipped machine.

Donation-/Sponsor-Notes:

  • Crest, as a public machine, was donated to the Debian Project by Anton Emmerfors and is hosted by the Biophysics department of the University of Duesseldorf. It is named for Klaus 'crest' Burkert. (more..)
  • Akire was donated for usage by Matthias Kröniger (Sichtwerk Augenoptik, Nürnberg) and is hosted by noris network AG, Nürnberg.
  • Arrakis and Spice are donated for usage by Ingo Jürgensmann.
  • jt7 was donated by Tuomas Vainikka.
  • kiivi was donated by Meelis Roos and is hosted by Cybernetica AS in Tartu, Estonia.
